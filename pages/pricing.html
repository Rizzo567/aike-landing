Owl Credits

Understanding your credits

Credits are the fuel for Owl — your AI operator. Every time you ask Owl a question, generate an image, or run an advanced model, a small number of credits is consumed. They reset monthly with your plan.

Credits reset monthly

Your full credit balance is restored on the same day each month. Unused credits do not roll over — fresh slate, every cycle.

Cost depends on the model

Light models (Gemini Flash) cost 1–2 credits per message. More powerful models (Claude, GPT-4.1) cost 3. You choose the right power level.

Track usage in real time

Owl shows your remaining credits in the dashboard. You'll always know how much is left before making any high-value request.

Common questions

What happens when I run out of credits?

Owl will notify you when you're running low. You won't be cut off abruptly — you'll see your remaining balance in real time and can upgrade or wait for the monthly reset.

Can I use any AI model I want?

Yes. All plans have access to the full model library — Gemini Flash, GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, Gemini Pro, and Claude Sonnet. You choose per message, based on what the task requires.

Do unused credits carry over?

No. Credits reset monthly. This keeps the system lean and predictable — you always start fresh with your full allocation.

Is this a subscription or one-time payment?

The subscription covers your monthly credit allocation and Aike infrastructure access. The automation architecture we build for you is a permanent asset you own forever.
