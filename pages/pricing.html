Common questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Basic websites are typically ready in 5–7 days. Professional and Custom projects depend on scope — we'll give you a clear timeline after the discovery call.

Do I own the website when it's done?

Yes. Once delivered, the website is entirely yours — code, assets, domain, hosting. No lock-in, no monthly fees unless you choose ongoing support.

Can you integrate automations into my website?

Absolutely. Custom Solution packages include deep integration with Aike automation systems — lead capture, CRM connections, email flows, and more.

What if I need changes after launch?

We offer post-launch support and iteration packages. For ongoing changes, we can set up a simple CMS so you can update content yourself.
