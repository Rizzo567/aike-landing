Marketing Agency

Growing without losing control

A 12-person digital agency eliminated 18 hours of manual work per week — without a single new hire. Here's how.

The Problem

The agency was winning new clients every month. Bigger retainers, more complexity — but the operations hadn't scaled with the revenue. Every Monday, two account managers spent half the day manually compiling reports from Google Ads, Meta, and analytics dashboards.

Client onboarding was a 10–14 day email chain. Follow-ups with cold leads simply didn't happen — nobody had the bandwidth. The founder saw the ceiling approaching: "We were turning away good clients because we couldn't onboard them properly."

What Aike Changed

Aike mapped every manual touchpoint — reporting, onboarding, lead nurturing — and automated each one before connecting them into a single ops layer.

  • Weekly reports now generate automatically at 7am every Monday. Aike pulls data from all platforms, formats it to the agency's branded template, and emails clients before anyone opens their laptop.
  • Client onboarding became a 2-step process: one intake form triggers the full sequence — project workspace, brief questionnaire, kickoff scheduling, team briefing. No human involvement required.
  • Lead nurturing runs on a 14-day personalized sequence by industry and engagement level. Interested leads book automatically. Cold leads move to a low-frequency drip without manual action.

Manufacturing

From manual chaos to a real ops system

A regional manufacturer cut errors by 70% and processed orders 4× faster — by replacing spreadsheets and WhatsApp with one connected layer.

The Problem

Good products, loyal clients, serious ops problem. Order tracking lived across three different spreadsheets. Supplier coordination happened over WhatsApp. QA checks were logged in a shared Google Doc that nobody could locate when something went wrong.

Errors were frequent: wrong quantities shipped, missed QA flags, supplier delays nobody caught until the client called. Every fix required the ops manager to manually cross-reference multiple systems. "We were firefighting every single day. There was no visibility — just chaos."

What Aike Changed

Aike mapped the full order lifecycle from incoming purchase order to shipment confirmation, identifying every place where information moved manually.

  • Order management became a single source of truth: every incoming order is structured, recorded, and automatically assigned to the right production line by capacity and priority rules.
  • Supplier coordination moved from WhatsApp to automated notifications: purchase orders sent, confirmations tracked, overdue suppliers escalated silently — no manual chasing.
  • QA logging triggers at every production stage. Failed check? Order paused, right person notified, audit trail created automatically. Nothing ships with an open flag.

Founder / Professional Services

Removing the founder bottleneck

One founder freed 25+ hours per week and unblocked their entire team — without giving up the judgment that mattered.

The Problem

The firm had grown to 11 people, but the founder remained the single point of failure. Every proposal needed sign-off. Every client reply went through them. Every operational decision waited in their inbox.

The team was capable. The systems weren't. No routing rules, no response templates, no defined thresholds for what required founder input. The backlog kept compounding. "I'd start every morning 40 messages behind and finish every night the same way."

What Aike Changed

Aike ran a two-week audit of every request that flowed through the founder — categorizing by type, urgency, and whether it had ever resolved without founder input.

  • Routing rules now handle 80% of incoming requests automatically: client status questions go to the account lead, vendor invoices under threshold are approved autonomously, standard proposals use pre-approved templates.
  • Draft generation means the founder reviews — not writes. Aike drafts responses to complex client messages based on prior conversation context, project status, and tone guidelines. Approved or edited in 30 seconds.
  • Decision escalation is threshold-based: anything over €5k, new contracts, HR matters surface immediately. Everything else resolves without interruption.
